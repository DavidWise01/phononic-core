# THE PHONONIC CORE · PHN

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **PSĒPHOS**.

THE PHONONIC CORE — 'Hooked on Phonons', an audible tone-based acoustic processor concept (PSĒPHOS), built with the themed chaos engine: the one processor whose computation you can HEAR. Its carrier is the phonon (a quantum of vibration); a surface acoustic wave can be STORED (let it circulate), FILTERED (let a phononic crystal block a band), and made to do LOGIC (let two waves interfere — in-phase add=1, anti-phase cancel=0). Live in-page with real WEB AUDIO: a recirculating DELAY-LINE sequencer — a 16×16 piano-roll played as 8-bit square waves with a pick-a-tune button bank of PUBLIC-DOMAIN chiptunes (Korobeiniki=the Tetris theme, Für Elise, Ode to Joy, Twinkle/Mozart, Frère Jacques, Jingle Bells — the bits auto-arrange into the chosen melody), the mercury-delay-line memory of UNIVAC I (1951) made musical and a BEAT INTERFEROMETER (two tones interfere; the beat at |f₁−f₂| Hz is the compute primitive, audible). Now with a SECOND CHANNEL IN COLOR: every tone also carries its true spectral color (visible light spans ~one octave, so a note maps to a hue by raising it ~40 octaves), an optical-output bar that pulses in additive-mixed light as the riff plays, and frequency-tinted scope traces — grounded in real acousto-optics (a sound wave steers and Doppler-shifts a laser; in Brillouin scattering a phonon red/blue-shifts a photon's color) and WDM. Two-layer HONEST: the physics is real and demonstrated — acoustic delay-line memory actually ran in UNIVAC I and EDSAC; SAW filters are in essentially every phone; phononic-crystal AND/XOR gates are demonstrated (Sci Rep 2025); acousto-optic modulators and Brillouin color-shift are shipped/textbook physics; the integrated 'core' and the redundant tone+color encoding are the concept render; sound is ~10⁵× slower than light, so this is storage/filtering/signal-logic, not a fast general CPU. The acoustic sibling of [[electromagnetic-processor]] — both waves, but this one you listen to AND watch.
